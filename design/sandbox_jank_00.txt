just another mine craft clone, but with procedural character creation that needs mined world resources to customise character, and automation via social connections

5 elements x 12 zodiack are the minerals mined and combine them to create/ upgrade character

world moves around occasionally unless you have ownership/ defined link to node
can capture/ raid of node ownership (destroy central crystal, or take central crystal and get to boundrary of region, whole zone turned into combat arena)
