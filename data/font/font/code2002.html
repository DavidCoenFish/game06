Code2002, a Plane 2 Unicode-based Font



Code2002 is a Plane 2 Unicode-based font (TTF).

The font is correctly encoded for Plane 2 per the latest TTF/OTF specifications. (As of twenty years ago.) Both Apple and Microsoft now include specifications for cmap format 12 at their respective typography web sites.

In this version, the coverage is still incomplete and many of the glyphs are rough. Just like all the previous versions. Maybe it keeps getting a little better each time, though.

Version 0.922 adds a few more glyphs covering a few more characters and adds complete support for the new CJK Extension I.

Download the newest version! (0.922) CODE2002.ZIP Update uploaded 2023-09-13

Code2002 is freeware, but must not be altered in any way.
Embedding is enabled.

As with Code2000 and Code2001, Code2002 relies on font-smoothing (anti-aliasing) for screen display, it does not contain hinting instructions.

Adjusting ClearType settings;
On Windows 7, [Start] - [Control Panel] - [Display] - [Adjust ClearType text].
